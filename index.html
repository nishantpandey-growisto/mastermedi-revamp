1. Speed & Performance — Fully Aligned

Speed is the #1 design principle of our plan. Our theme choice (Shapes) has the highest speed rating in its class. Our targets are set to match or beat your direct competitors — not aspirational numbers that ignore the reality of apps, custom features, and rich visual content.

ⓘ Why not 90+ mobile? Every Shopify store's PageSpeed score is a trade-off between features and speed. Each app (Klaviyo, Judge.me, Tidio, etc.) adds 50-200KB of JavaScript. Premium visual design (animations, custom fonts, hero videos) adds render weight. A 90+ mobile score is only realistic for stores with zero third-party apps and minimal visual design — which defeats the purpose of a premium oral care brand. Our target of 50+ mobile / 60+ desktop means we will outperform every competitor in your category while still delivering the rich experience your brand needs.
