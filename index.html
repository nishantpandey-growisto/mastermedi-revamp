Current Challenges

Frontend issues we spotted + backend operations gaps you’ve flagged — let’s align on priorities

Critical Issues

These are actively costing you customers and revenue

Critical

50% of Products Are Sold Out

8 out of 16 products show “Sold Out” — including entire categories (Acne Patches, Dietary Supplements, all Teeth Whitening). Visitors arriving from TikTok ads or organic search for these products have zero opportunity to buy.

Impact: Wasted ad spend, immediate bounces, damaged brand perception
Critical

No Trust Elements for Health Products

No FDA compliance badges, no ADA endorsements, no manufacturing certifications (GMP/ISO), no third-party lab testing badges, no ingredient safety certifications. For a health/oral care brand selling in the US, this is a major conversion blocker.

Impact: Health-conscious buyers won’t purchase without trust signals
Critical

Cluttered Design & Visual Overload

Too many visual elements competing for attention. Announcement bar text repeated 10 times. 5-slide hero banner. No clear visual hierarchy. The design feels more like a generic ecommerce store than a premium oral care brand.

Impact: High bounce rate, poor brand impression vs competitors like marvis.com

High Priority Issues

Significant friction points hurting conversion

High

Slow Page Load (Likely 30-50 Mobile Score)

Multiple third-party scripts (GA, FB Pixel, Google Ads, AppIO Reviews, Pumper, hCaptcha), 5-slide hero carousel, product galleries with 7-29 images each. For TikTok traffic that’s mobile-first, this kills conversion.

Impact: Every 1-second delay = ~7% conversion loss
High

Reviews Not Rendering on Product Pages

Star ratings and review counts show on collection pages (4.2-4.9 stars, 4-98 reviews), but actual review content doesn’t render on product detail pages. Social proof is the #1 conversion driver for health products.

Impact: Lost social proof at the critical purchase decision moment
High

No Shipping Cost Transparency

Shipping policy page mentions 3 tiers (Economy, Standard, Express) but provides zero pricing. Customers don’t know shipping costs until checkout — a major cart abandonment driver.

Impact: Surprise costs at checkout = high cart abandonment
High

Minimal Product Information

Product descriptions are 2-3 sentences. No ingredients list on toothpaste/pulling oil (critical for health products). No usage instructions. No FAQ sections. Health-conscious buyers need detailed information to feel confident purchasing.

Impact: Insufficient info = lost sales on health products

Backend & Operations Gaps

You mentioned the frontend was set up okay — it’s the backend that’s been the challenge. Here’s what we see.

Critical

No Inventory Management System

With 50% of products showing “Sold Out” and no automated stock management, there’s no visibility into reorder points, stock velocity, or multi-warehouse inventory. Currently relying on manual Shopify inventory counts with no alerts or automation.

Impact: Chronic stockouts, no demand forecasting, lost sales on popular items
Critical

No Order Management / Fulfillment Workflow

No OMS (Order Management System) beyond Shopify’s basic orders page. No automated order routing, no fulfillment status tracking, no packing slip automation. As you scale to India, you’ll need order routing by geography (US warehouse vs India warehouse).

Impact: Manual order processing doesn’t scale; multi-country fulfillment needs automation
Critical

App Stack Not Set Up

Critical apps are either missing or misconfigured: reviews app not rendering, no email/SMS marketing flows (abandoned cart, post-purchase, win-back), no shipping calculator, no delivery estimation, no loyalty program, no analytics beyond basic GA. These are the revenue drivers.

Impact: Zero retention marketing, zero post-purchase engagement, no automated revenue recovery
High

No Multi-Country Backend Infrastructure

India expansion requires: Shopify Markets setup, geo-IP routing, separate product catalogs by market, INR pricing, Indian payment gateways (Razorpay/PayU), Indian shipping partners (Shiprocket/Delhivery), GST invoicing. None of this is in place.

Impact: India launch is blocked until backend infrastructure is built
High

Shipping & Logistics Not Configured

No shipping rate transparency for customers. No integrated shipping solution for rate calculation, label generation, or tracking. For India, you’ll need domestic logistics partners. For US, need to optimize current shipping setup with real-time rates.

Impact: High cart abandonment from hidden shipping costs; no tracking experience
High

No Analytics & Tracking Setup

Basic Google Analytics likely installed but ecommerce events (add_to_cart, begin_checkout, purchase, etc.) may not be properly configured. No server-side tracking. No heatmaps or session recordings. Without proper tracking, you can’t measure what’s working.

Impact: Flying blind — no data to optimize ad spend, conversion, or customer journey

Medium Priority Issues

Important improvements for a complete revamp

Medium

No Multi-Country / India Support

USD only. No geo-targeting, no country selector, no multi-currency. The store is configured as an Indian Shopify store but operates US-only. India expansion needs Shopify Markets, separate catalogs, and geo-routing.

Medium

Broken Links & UX Issues

FAQs footer link goes to “#” (broken). Cookie Policy link broken. Affiliate page CTA links to “#”. URL typo: /collections/dietry-supplement. Shipping policy on confusing URL (/return-policy-1).

Medium

Limited Payment Options

Only PayPal visible in footer. No credit card logos, no Apple Pay, no Google Pay, no Shop Pay badges. For US customers, this limits checkout confidence and options.

Medium

No Contact Transparency

Only an email (marketing@mastermedi.co) and a basic form. No phone number, no live chat, no WhatsApp, no physical address. For health products, customers want to know there’s a real company behind the brand.

Medium

Blog Abandoned Since August 2024

Only 5 blog posts, all from Aug 2024 (6+ months stale). For oral care/health products, educational content (ingredient science, usage guides, oral health tips) drives organic traffic and builds authority.
