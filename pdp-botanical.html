The Details

Description

The MasterMedi Stainless Steel Tongue Scraper is an Ayurveda-inspired oral care tool designed for the modern ritual. Crafted from medical-grade 304 stainless steel, it gently and effectively removes the white coating (ama) from your tongue surface, reducing bacteria by up to 75% in a single use.


Unlike plastic alternatives, our stainless steel scraper is built to last a lifetime, will not harbor bacteria, and can be sterilized at high temperatures. The ergonomic U-shape fits comfortably against the tongue's natural curve, reaching the back without triggering the gag reflex.

How to Use
  • Use first thing in the morning, ideally after oil pulling and before brushing
  • Hold both handles firmly with each hand
  • Extend your tongue and place the curved edge at the back
  • Gently pull forward in one smooth stroke — do not press too hard
  • Rinse the scraper under running water between each stroke
  • Repeat 5–7 times until the tongue looks pink and clean
  • Rinse scraper thoroughly and store in a dry place
Care Instructions
  • Rinse with warm water after each use
  • For deep cleaning, soak in boiling water for 2–3 minutes once a week
  • Alternatively, wipe with rubbing alcohol or hydrogen peroxide
  • Pat dry and store in the included cotton pouch
  • Do not use abrasive cleaners or steel wool
  • Stainless steel will not rust or corrode under normal use
