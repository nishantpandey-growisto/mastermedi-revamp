Common Questions

Product FAQs

How do I use a tongue scraper?
Simply place the rounded edge at the back of your tongue, and gently pull forward toward the tip. Rinse the scraper after each pass. Repeat 3-5 times, or until your tongue feels clean. Best used in the morning before brushing.
Stainless steel vs copper — which should I choose?
Stainless steel is more durable and easier to maintain. Copper has natural antimicrobial properties rooted in Ayurvedic tradition but requires occasional polishing. Both are equally effective at removing tongue coating.
How do I clean my tongue scraper?
Rinse with warm water after each use. For a deeper clean, wash with soap and water or place in your dishwasher (top rack). Stainless steel scrapers can also be sterilized with boiling water.
Is this safe for sensitive tongues?
Absolutely. The mirror-polished edges are smooth and gentle. Start with light pressure and gradually increase as you become comfortable. Our U-shape design distributes pressure evenly to prevent gagging.
